Hi. Welcome to Re-Nova.

Nova is the inner explosion in a star that makes it glow, and renovation aims at the betterment of the society.
Re-Nova is all about listening to our hearts, standing up together with an inner explosion (a call from our soul) and renovating the world into a better place.

Re-Nova Studio

We use a Yamaha PSR E343 keyboard, Behringer XNYX 302 USB Mixer, Shure SV100 microphone and VSTs for production

Stagelight Artist

We are part of the Open Labs' Stagelight Artist Community since 2013. We have recorded many tracks on this amazing DAW.

Soundbank

Explorer is our in-progress soundbank. It consists of various tracks and sounds. It is an open source project. Click here to be a part.

Original Soundtracks

Re-Nova OSTs is our project on movie background scores. We have made tracks that have been featured in some short videos.

Jam & Fun

Our portable studio is filled with new beats and stuff almost all the time. Session contains tracks that were made during experiments.
